Sights

Enjoy the ancient and architecial monuments of over 800 years of moving history.

TV Tower

The trademark of Berlin is undoubtly the television tower. The total of 368 meter were build in 1969 at the Alexander Platz.

learn more

Memorial Church

The Kaiser-Friedrich-Memorial-Church is a statement for the dark history of germany. The building was bombed during world war II, only the clocktower remained.

learn more

Brandenburg Gate

Build between 1789 and 1793 by the prussian king Friederich Wilhelm, this monument stands for the formal seperation of the city.

Travel

Berlin is reachable via several transport possibilites. Book your journey directly here.

Train

The "Deutsche Bahn" offers highly comfortable train-waggons and with the InterCity trains, the time flies at highspeed. Book your tickets here.

Bus

Germany is known for it's "Autobahn". Take a seat in one of many firstclass coaches and enjoy the view across the country. Berlin's main bus-station welcomes you.

Airplane

Berlin used to have three airports of which two were inner city. After 14 years of building, with only 9 years delay, the new airport opened it's gates for international connections.

Bike

All around and through Berlin are endless kilometers of bike-routes you can enjoy. Get saddled and download your route-maps here.
